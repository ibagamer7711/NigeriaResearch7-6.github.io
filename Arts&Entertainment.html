Arts & Entertainment:



Nigeria has a rich artistic heritage, including traditional and contemporary art forms. For example, naturally produced statues called Ife., Ikype masks, statue of Queen Elizabeth II. Music and dance are essential to Nigerian culture. Each group has its own specialities. Traditional instruments include flutes, trumpets, musical bows, xylophone, wooden clappers, and drums. Music is used to celebrate special moments.
