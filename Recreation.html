Recreation:



Nigerians enjoy various sports activities. Sports such as boxing, football, and tennis are extremely popular in Nigeria. While tennis and boxing are very popular, most Nigerians are fans of football and play it in their free time. In addition to football, Nigerians also enjoy playing basketball, a sport which was popularized by a basketball player named Hakeem Olajuwon.
