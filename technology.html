Africa has a total of seven technological companies valued at over $1 billion with 4 of them located in Nigeria. The US Ambassador to Nigeria, in her speech at the 2021 Tech Summit said that the Nigerian tech industry will encourage the creation of an environment that will launch more global businesses and provide to a more successful future for all its citizens.
