Resources:



Nigeria is valued for many of its resources, but the most profitable one is petroleum, which is the most important source of government export revenue. Accounting for atleast 86% of the Nigeria's export revenue. Some of its other natural resources include natural gas, tin, iron ore, coal, limestone, niobium, lead, zinc, and arable(farming) land. The oil and gas sector accounts for about 10 per cent of the country's Gross Domestic Product(GDP).
