Education:



Nigeria has the highest amount of children out of school in the world with 10.6 million kids not recieving education. The schooling and education system is 6-3-3-4. 6 years being primary school, 3 years of junior high school, 3 years of senior(highschool)education and 4 years of tertiary education.
