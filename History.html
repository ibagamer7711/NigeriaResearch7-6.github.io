History:



Nigeria has a lot of historic empires and cultures compared to other countries in Africa. Nigeria has a history that dates as far back as 11,000 BC when ancient African tribes inhabited the current Nigerian territory. The Benin was the most prominent empire In the region before the British arrived.It was ruled by the Oba of Benin, the ruler of the empire. Originally British-ruled, Nigeria was colonized by Great Britain until 1960 when it became an independent country.
